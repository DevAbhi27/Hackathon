Sri Venkateswara, Tirumala

One of the richest and most visited temples in the world, atop the sacred Seven Hills.

Brief History

The Seven Hills

The Tirumala temple is dedicated to Lord Venkateswara, a form of Vishnu. The shrine’s origins trace back over a millennium, with royal patronage from the Pallavas, Cholas, and Vijayanagara rulers.

Traditions & Offerings

Famed for the Tirupati Laddu and tonsure ritual, the temple symbolizes surrender and devotion. Strict darshan protocols ensure smooth flow for millions of pilgrims.

Fun & Sacred Activities


Suprabhata Seva

Early morning darshan and chants awakening the Lord—book in advance.

Duration: ~1 hour

Prasadam Tasting

Taste the iconic Tirupati Laddu and other temple prasads freshly prepared.

Duration: 30 mins

Walk the Footpath (Srivari Mettu)

Pilgrim trek through forests to reach the hilltop—spiritually uplifting.

Duration: 1.5–3 hours
