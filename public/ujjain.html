Mahakaleshwar Jyotirlinga

Seek blessings at one of the twelve sacred Jyotirlingas in the ancient city of Ujjain.

Sacred Heritage & History

Ancient Roots

Mahakaleshwar, one of the twelve Jyotirlingas of Lord Shiva, is revered for its unique south-facing idol (Dakshinmukhi) and powerful spiritual aura. The temple finds mention in the Puranas and has been a center of Shaivism for centuries.

City of Time

Ujjain, known as the city of Mahakaal (the Lord of Time), sits on the banks of the sacred Shipra River. It is one of the seven Moksha-puris and a principal site of the Kumbh Mela, drawing millions of pilgrims.

Bhasma Aarti

The pre-dawn Bhasma Aarti is the temple’s most iconic ritual, where sacred ash is offered to Lord Shiva—a deeply transformative experience for devotees.

Fun & Sacred Activities


Attend Bhasma Aarti

Witness the legendary Bhasma Aarti at dawn. Pre-registration is recommended due to limited capacity.

Duration: ~1 hour

Shipra Snan

Take a holy dip at Ram Ghat on the Shipra River, especially auspicious during festivals.

Duration: 30–60 mins

Evening Aarti

Experience bhajans and evening aarti in the temple complex—soothing and spiritually uplifting.

Duration: ~45 mins

Ujjain Heritage Walk

Explore ancient ghats, markets, and historic lanes around the temple with a local guide.

Duration: 1–2 hours

Nearest Must-Visit Temples

Harsiddhi Mata Temple

Shaktipeeth near Ram Ghat; famed for its unique deep-stambhs (lamp towers).

Kal Bhairav Temple

Dedicated to Bhairava, guardian deity of Ujjain—associated with unique offerings.

Mangalnath Temple

Regarded as the birthplace of Mars; popular for Navgraha pujas and stargazing.
