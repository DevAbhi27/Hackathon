Tanda Falls

Discover the serene beauty of Tanda Falls in the Lalganj region of Mirzapur, where nature's tranquility meets breathtaking landscapes.

Natural Beauty & Heritage

Serene Waterfall

Tanda Falls is a picturesque waterfall nestled in the Lalganj region of Mirzapur, known for its peaceful environment and natural beauty that attracts visitors seeking a tranquil retreat.

Vindhya Range Beauty

Located in the scenic Vindhya mountain range, Tanda Falls offers a perfect blend of natural splendor and peaceful surroundings, making it an ideal destination for nature lovers and adventure seekers.

Mirzapur's Natural Gem

As part of the historic Mirzapur district, Tanda Falls represents the region's rich natural heritage and serves as a gateway to exploring the diverse landscapes of eastern Uttar Pradesh.

Adventure & Nature Activities


Nature Photography

Capture the stunning beauty of the waterfall and surrounding landscapes with perfect lighting conditions throughout the day.

Duration: 1–2 hours

Trekking Adventure

Embark on scenic treks through the surrounding forests and hills to reach the waterfall and explore hidden natural gems.

Duration: 2–3 hours

Picnic & Relaxation

Enjoy a peaceful picnic by the waterfall with family and friends, surrounded by the soothing sounds of cascading water.

Duration: 2–4 hours

Nature Meditation

Find inner peace and tranquility in the serene environment, perfect for meditation and mindfulness practices.

Duration: 30–60 mins
