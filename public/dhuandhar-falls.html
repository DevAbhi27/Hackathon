Dhuandhar Falls

Witness the spectacular "smoky cascade" on the sacred Narmada River in Bhedaghat, where nature's power creates a mesmerizing misty spectacle.

Natural Wonder & Heritage

The Smoky Cascade

Dhuandhar Falls, meaning "smoky cascade," is a magnificent waterfall on the Narmada River that drops approximately 30 meters, creating a powerful roar and misty atmosphere that gives it its distinctive name.

Sacred Narmada

The falls are located on the sacred Narmada River, one of India's seven holy rivers. The Narmada is considered the lifeline of central India and holds immense spiritual significance for pilgrims and devotees.

Geological Marvel

Formed by the Narmada River cutting through the Vindhya mountain range, Dhuandhar Falls showcases the raw power of nature and the geological history of the region spanning millions of years.

Adventure & Exploration Activities


Cable Car Ride

Take an aerial view of the falls and surrounding marble cliffs with the ropeway facility. Operating from 10:30 AM to 5:30 PM.

Duration: ~15 mins

Marble Rocks Boating

Experience the magical boat ride through the 8km gorge flanked by towering marble cliffs, especially enchanting during moonlight.

Duration: 1–2 hours

Photography Session

Capture the misty beauty of the falls and the changing hues of marble rocks at different times of the day.

Duration: 30–60 mins

Narmada River Walk

Take a peaceful walk along the banks of the sacred Narmada River and enjoy the serene natural surroundings.

Duration: 1–2 hours

Nearby Must-Visit Attractions

Marble Rocks

Stunning 8km gorge with towering marble cliffs that change colors throughout the day. Best experienced during a boat ride.

Chausath Yogini Temple

Ancient temple dedicated to 64 forms of Goddess Durga, perched on a hill with panoramic views of the landscape.

Balancing Rock

Geological wonder featuring a massive rock precariously balanced on another, defying gravity and perfect for photography.

Madan Mahal Fort

11th-century fort built by Raja Madan Singh, offering historical insights and panoramic views of Jabalpur city.
